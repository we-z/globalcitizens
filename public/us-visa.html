Overview

Welcome to Global Citizens’ comprehensive guide to obtaining a US visa. We are committed to simplifying the process so that you can navigate your journey to the United States with ease and confidence.

Step 1: Determine the Visa Type

Identify the visa category that fits your purpose—be it tourism, business, study, or work. Each category has specific eligibility criteria and documentation requirements.

Step 2: Prepare Your Documentation

Gather all the necessary documents, which typically include:

  • A valid passport
  • Completed visa application form (e.g., DS-160 for non-immigrant visas)
  • Passport-sized photographs meeting US requirements
  • Supporting documents such as employment letters, bank statements, or invitation letters

Step 3: Schedule Your Visa Interview

Once your application and documentation are ready, schedule an interview at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate. Ensure you have all your documents in order for the interview.

Step 4: Attend the Visa Interview

During your interview, a consular officer will evaluate your application and ask questions about your travel plans. Answer honestly and confidently, providing any additional documents if requested.

Step 5: Visa Approval and Issuance

After a successful interview, your visa will be approved. The processing time may vary, so plan ahead and track your application status through the embassy's portal.
